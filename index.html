Homer

Your Trusted Platform for Filipino Home Service Professionals

A Digital Solution for the Filipino Home Service Sector

Introduction

  • Informal sector plays a significant role in the Philippine economy
  • Traditional hiring process for home services is inefficient
  • Opportunity to leverage technology for improved service delivery

Problem Statement

  • Lack of centralized platform for connecting clients and service providers
  • Inconsistent service quality and pricing
  • Safety concerns for both clients and service providers

Objectives

  • Develop a user-centered digital platform for home services
  • Improve efficiency in connecting clients with service providers
  • Enhance service quality through feedback and rating systems
  • Implement safety measures and secure payment gateways
  • Promote digital literacy

Significance of the Study

  • Addresses inefficiencies in the home service sector
  • Contributes to the formalization of informal labor
  • Enhances economic opportunities for service providers
  • Provides a model for digital innovation in emerging economies

Review of Related Literature

Digital Transformation in Home Services

  • Rise of on-demand service applications
  • Examples: Home Repairs (Oman), Domestic Android Application (India)
  • Key features: User-friendly interfaces, GPS integration, real-time feedback

Improving Service Quality and Accountability

  • Importance of feedback and rating systems
  • Examples: Mr. Right (India), SerbisyoPH (Philippines)
  • Benefits: Incentivizes high-quality service, fosters trust

Ensuring Safety and Security

  • Addressing risks in informal labor transactions
  • Key features: Identity verification, background checks

Promoting Financial Inclusion and Digital Literacy

  • Integrating secure payment systems

Global Trends and Local Adaptation

  • Adapting global best practices to Filipino context
  • Addressing local challenges: Internet access, digital literacy, trust
  • Opportunity for "Homer" to fill market gaps

Methodology

Methodology

Research Design

  • Descriptive and developmental research approach
  • Rapid Application Development (RAD) methodology

Scope and Limitations

  • Focus on urban areas in the Philippines
  • Limited to smartphone users with internet access
  • May not fully represent rural or less technologically advanced regions

Tools for Web App and PWA Development

  • Progressive Web Application (PWA) technology
  • Web technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
  • Geolocation services
  • Real-time scheduling systems
  • Secure payment gateways

Expected Outcomes

  • Improved efficiency in home service transactions
  • Enhanced income stability for service providers
  • Better service quality and accountability
  • Increased safety and security for all users
  • Promotion of digital literacy and financial inclusion
